# Schritte plus: A German Language Course for Beginners
 
Schritte plus is a series of textbooks and workbooks for learning German as a foreign language. It is designed for adults who live or want to live in a German-speaking country. The series covers the levels A1 to B1 of the Common European Framework of Reference for Languages (CEFR) and follows the guidelines of the German Federal Ministry of the Interior's curriculum for integration courses.
 
The books combine language learning with cultural and everyday topics, such as family, work, health, shopping, and leisure. They also offer interactive exercises, audio-CDs, vocabulary lists, transcripts, and solutions. The books can be used in classroom settings or for self-study.
